Biography

I'm currently an Assistant Professor in Prof. Fu Xiao's Group in School of Computer Science, Nanjing University of Posts and Telecommunications, China. I obtained my Ph.D. degree from Department of Computer Science and Technology in Nanjing University in December 2021. Before that, I am a Ph.D. candidate in LANDs (led by Prof. Guihai Chen, IEEE fellow), under the supervision of Prof. Jiaqi Zheng and Prof. Wanchun Dou from September 2017 to December 2021. I am a member of ACM, IEEE and CCF.

Research Interests

I focus on investigating the technological deficiencies in computer networks. I aim to address practical network problems and improve the system performance by using mathematical models and buliding systems. My research inserests are:

  • Data center network: Priority-based flow control, flow scheduling, RDMA congestion control, lossless transmission protocal.

  • Edge computing network: Jointly resource optimazation, federated learning application.

Prospective Students

I am always looking for self-movitated students (Master/Undergraduates). Students with strong coding ability and/or mathematical background (EE, math) are highly encouraged. I have two master positions every year (每年可招收2名硕士研究生). Drop me an email if you are interested in working with me.
